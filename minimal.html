About

A Flutter / mobile developer based in Da Nang, Vietnam — for 6+ years the lead or sole developer on 30+ production apps across mobility, safety, health, sport and social.

At Digital Unicorn I lead the mobile side of the DUOCT “Octopus” super-app, and I wrote the shared Flutter base architecture that 20+ of our apps are built on — turning “a few weeks of setup” into “start today.”

I'm not a fan of over-engineering. I'd rather ship something that works, feels right, and won't be a nightmare to maintain in six months — clean MVVM, real-time that doesn't stutter, payments that don't break, builds that hit the store on time.
