The problem
Motivation is easy. Consistency is the hard part.

Most training apps hand you a video and a shrug. No plan that fits you, no record of your one-rep max, no test to prove you actually got stronger — so the gains, and the habit, quietly fade.

The journey
Spaurt coaches you across four pillars.

Rep- and time-based routines and a full routine library. 1-rep-max & PR tracking, physical tests and clear goals. Statistics that show the curve, and a calendar that keeps you honest — hybrid, personalised sports training that adapts to you.
