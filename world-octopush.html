The problem
Customers write from everywhere.

SMS here, a chat there, a missed message in a third tab. For a team handling real customers, conversations scatter across channels — and a reply that comes an hour late is a reply that comes too late.

The journey
Octopush pulls them into one thread.

A customer-conversation messenger for the Octopush (Marseille) multichannel / SMS platform: the whole team handles every conversation and live chat from a single app, with push notifications so nothing slips — replies fast, from one place.
