The problem
A normal day takes a dozen apps.

One to get a ride. One for food, another for flowers, a third for a courier. A fourth to pay. Each with its own login, its own wallet, its own waiting — a whole drawer of icons just to get through a Tuesday.

The journey
Octopus folds the city into one app.

Book a ride. Order across food, flowers, high-tech and home services. Send a courier and track the dispatch. Pay from a single wallet. Underneath: separate customer and driver apps, real-time matching, earnings and payouts, and premium perks — the flagship super-app I lead at Digital Unicorn.
