The problem
You can't change what you can't see.

Carbon is invisible. A meal, a commute, a delivery — each one has a number, but nobody shows it to you. So "go greener" stays a vague good intention, with no scoreboard and no reason to start today.

The journey
Nila puts a number on every day.

Measure the CO₂ impact of daily tasks. Track weekly emissions and a carbon score that actually moves. Complete green challenges to win gifts from partners. And share your eco wins on a social feed — because progress is easier when it's a game you can see.
