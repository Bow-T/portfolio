The problem
When something goes wrong, it goes wrong fast.

And usually you're alone, on a street you half-know, with no idea where the nearest safe place is or how to tell someone you trust — quickly, without fumbling. In those seconds, information is everything.

The journey
ProtectU turns panic into a plan.

An interactive incident map shows what's happening around you in real time. It guides you to the nearest safe place. It shares your live location and opens secure messaging to your trusted contacts in a tap — a French citizen-safety app built to see, understand and act.
