The problem
Everyone has a street they don't take after dark.

The badly lit shortcut. The corner that just feels wrong. That knowledge lives in people's heads — scattered, unshared, and usually learned the hard way. A whole city's sense of safety, and none of it on a map.

The journey
So SecuMap puts it on the map — together.

An interactive map of the whole country, updated in real time by thousands of people, marking the places to avoid: poorly lit streets, reported incidents, corners known to be unsafe. Anyone can flag a danger — anonymously — to warn whoever walks there next. And when a hazard lands on your route, the app quietly recalculates a safer way around it. Free, anonymous, and entirely French.
