The problem
The seat is empty. The fees are not.

Carpooling should be the easy win — split the cost of a drive you're already making. But sign-up friction, slow matching and a service cut on every trip quietly kill the habit before it starts.

The journey
Ynstant makes a ride a 30-second thing.

Post a ride in under 30 seconds. Get bookings from verified passengers, or book instantly and set ride alerts. Real-time matching, in-app messaging and distance-based pricing — daily carpooling across France, with zero service fees.
